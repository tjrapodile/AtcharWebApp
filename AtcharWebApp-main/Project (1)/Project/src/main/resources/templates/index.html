About the business

  • The business was established in 2016. It dawned upon me when I was sitting at home rembering my childhood eating sweet and sour artchaar at my grandmother's house. I decided to recreate the recipe with my cousins. I realised that this is such a unique taste so I decided to try and sell the atchaar in the community and it went great. I then decided to experiment with more recipes like chilli recepies and I saw how well people loved them so that is where it all began.
